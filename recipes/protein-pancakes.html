Protein Pancakes

Description

These protein packed pancakes are perfect for a tasty breakfast without any of the guilt.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup cottage Cheese
  • 1 Cup Rolled Oats
  • 5 Large Eggs
  • 2 tsp. Cinnamon
  • 1 Tbsp. Maple Syrup
  • 2 Tbsp. Coconut Oil

Steps

  1. Place all ingredients into a blender or food processor and blitz together until smooth.

  2. Heat 1/4 of the coconut oil in a large frying pan over low-medium heat, pour about 1/4 cup of batter per pancake, and repeat until the pan is filled.

  3. Cook until you start to see bubbles on the surface of the pancakes, about 1-2 minutes. Then flip and cook for another minute or until golden brown on both sides.

  4. Repeat this process until all the batter has been used. Top the pancakes with your favourite toppings.
