Purple Power Smoothie

Description

This is a super easy, quick, high-protein smoothie. Perfect for breakfast or on the go snacking.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Almond Milk
  • 1 Cup Frozen Mixed Berries
  • 1 Banana
  • 1 Medium Beetroot, Cooked and Grated
  • 25g Vanilla Whey

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients into a blender. Pulse until smooth then pour into a glass and serve straight immediately.
